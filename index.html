How it works

Three steps, one of them yours.

The first two happen quietly in the background. The third is the only one that matters.

01

Read

An inquiry lands in your inbox or contact form. Setlync reads it once, end to end, and pulls the date, venue, headcount, and anything else worth knowing.

02

Draft

A reply is prepared in your voice. Missing details are flagged in the margin. Conflicting dates raise their hand.

03

Review

You see the draft. Edit, send, or ignore. Nothing leaves your account without you. You stay the photographer your couples chose.
